How much do you charge per lesson?

I charge £15 per lesson, per child. If parents book numerous sessions in blocks, discounts can be allocated. Please e-mail to enquire about the options that are available to you.

How many students per class?

I am aim to maintain small class sizes of three to four students. Classes will be based on ability and age group to ensure students can participate and engage.

How can I make payments?

Given the current climate, I can only accept PayPal or Bank Transfer payments. This can be discussed when you make your first booking.

Do you guarantee results?

No tutor can guarantee results. A student must be dedicated to succeed and must have the willingness to participate in their own learning.
